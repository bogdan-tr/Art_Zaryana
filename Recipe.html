Dalgona Coffee

A unique, creamy, whipped coffee

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 1 cup milk (hot or cold)

Instructions:

  1. Mix coffee, sugar, and hot water.
  2. Whip until creamy
  3. Pour milk into a glass.
  4. Spoon the whipped coffee on top.
  5. Stir before drinking.

Bulletproof Coffee

A tasty, buttery delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black coffee
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp MCT oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix coffee, butter, and MCT oil.
  2. Put into a blender.
  3. Blend for 30s until smooth.

Cappuccino

The tradional Italian classic

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp dark roast coffee
  • 4oz water
  • 4oz milk

Instructions:

  1. Prepare double shot of espresso.
  2. Foam the milk to 2x its size.
  3. Top the espresso with the foam.

Irish Coffee

The harmonious coupling of coffee and whiskey

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz hot coffee
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1.5 oz Irish whiskey
  • Fresh whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Fill mug with coffee.
  2. Add sugars and whiskey.
  3. Mix it.
  4. Top with whipped cream

Mocha

Coupling the worlds of coffee and chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • 0.25 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream
  • Whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Brew hot coffee
  2. Mix and heat the water, cocoa and sugar
  3. Stir in coffee and milk
  4. Heat until steaming
  5. Top with whipped cream

Affogato

"Drowning" ice cream in coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot esspresso
  • 2 scoops vanilla gelato
  • Shaved dark chocolate (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare esspresso
  2. Add ice cream into a glass
  3. Pour esspresso over glass
  4. Top with the shaved dark chocolate

Cold Brew

It's coffee, but iced and refreshing

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole coffee beans
  • 4 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Grind coffee beans
  2. Combine the grinded beans and water
  3. Steep overnight
  4. Strain the coffee
  5. Serve with ice and milk

Mazagran

An exotic coffee lemonade from Algeria

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots esspresso
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4-5tbsp lemon joice
  • Crushed ice
  • Lemon slices

Instructions:

  1. Put everything into a container
  2. Mix and shake vigorously
  3. Serve with lemon slices
